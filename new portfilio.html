About Me

Dedicated Educator,
Passionate Trainer

I am Rajal Makwana, an ITI COPA (Computer Operator and Programming Assistant) Trainer based in Gujarat, India. With over a decade of experience in vocational computer education, I specialize in making technology accessible and practical for every student — regardless of their background.

My teaching philosophy centers on hands-on practice, real-world applications, and building strong digital foundations. I take pride in guiding students from basic computer literacy to job-ready proficiency in data entry, office applications, and fundamental programming.
